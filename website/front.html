About Kavach

Kavach is a revolutionary mobile application that addresses the limitations of existing SOS technologies. Our mission is to provide a reliable and discreet solution for individuals in distress, enabling them to send SOS alerts efficiently and anonymously.

With Kavach, you can set up a personalized SOS pattern on a custom lock screen interface. Additionally, the application offers voice recognition and fingerprint recognition features, ensuring quick and discreet activation of the SOS alert.
